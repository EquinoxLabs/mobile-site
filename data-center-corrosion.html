Signs of Data Center Corrosion are:
  • Failures of Discrete Electronic Components & PCB Boards
  • Sporadic Circuit Failure
  • Lower Energy Efficiency IT Equipment
  • Replacement of Damaged Electronic Components
  • Incorrect and Loss of Data
  • Increase in Data Center’s Maintenance Costs

Factors causing corrosion

“Corrosion” in electronic equipment refers to reactions between Copper and Silver surfaces of the IT equipment with its immediate surroundings. As the chemical reaction continues, pitting and metal loss can occur.

What are Corrosive Gases?

Acidic Gases such as hydrogen sulfide (H2S), Sulfur Oxides, Chlorine and Nitrogen Oxides. Acidic gases are typically the most harmful and require better monitoring.
