ABOUT US

We have 14+ years of experience in Food, Water & Air Testing services and a leader in Food Safety Auditing. We are an FSSAI Approved, NABL Accredited Lab with a PAN – India presence.

We use the latest technology to deliver best in class services for a 360° approach to Safety & Compliance. We have delivered 50,000+ solutions in Testing and Compliance. We have trained 40,000+ people on Food Safety, FSSAI & Environmental safety.

Our company has over 250 in-house experts catering to 14,000+ Clients across Food and Corporate sector. Our organization has teamed up with several Food SMEs and conglomerates, along with Corporate for ensuring complete Safety and Health– Audits, Testing, Consultations, and FoSTaC – to name a few.

It all started with an idea to provide a one-stop Testing and FSSAI Compliance solutions, using the latest technologies and innovations. With time, Equinox Labs has grown from just a Testing Laboratory to a Food Safety Management, largely involved with FSSAI in their decision-making process on pivotal regulations. The company’s credibility can be attributed to the invaluable benefits the clients have achieved by associating with Equinox Labs.

Equinox Labs’ experience of working with diverse food businesses, has given us an edge. From Corporate to Caterers, Hotels to Food Manufacturers, Institutes to Industries – The company’s in-house Testing and Compliance Experts have provided State-of-The-Art Solutions for a continued Safety and Health Management.

"Competency, expertise and proficiency in Food, Water & Environment testing is our forte"
